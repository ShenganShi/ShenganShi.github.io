Our Services

Construction Superintendents are essential on-site leaders who oversee every detail of your project, from start to finish. They ensure your project stays on schedule, within budget, and meets the highest quality standards.

Construction Superintendents

At Modern Consulting, our expert construction superintendents make your project easier. From daily site visits to handling inspections and ensuring safety, we take care of the details so you can focus on the big picture. Part-time or full-time, we’ll keep your project on track, compliant, and stress-free.

Site Safety Manager/Coordinators

At Modern Consulting, our Site Safety Managers and Coordinators are your partners in creating safer job sites. From tailoring safety plans to regular inspections and on-site training, we focus on preventing accidents before they happen. With our expert guidance, you can ensure compliance, protect your team, and keep your project running smoothly and safely.

Regulatory Compliance and Code Adherence

Keeping up with safety regulations can be overwhelming, but Modern Consulting makes it simple. Our team ensures your project meets NYC DOB and OSHA codes, helping you avoid shutdowns and fines. We stay on top of changing rules and address compliance issues before they become problems. If an accident happens, we investigate, find the root cause, and help you create a safer site.

Safety Program Development and Training

Safety doesn’t just happen—it starts with a plan. Modern Consulting creates tailored safety programs and conducts audits to catch issues early, saving you time and money. Our training goes beyond the basics, covering OSHA standards and emergency response. With our help, your team will work smarter, safer, and more confidently every day.
